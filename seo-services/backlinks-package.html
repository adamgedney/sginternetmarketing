Getting high quality backlinks is the most complicated and time consuming part of a good SEO campaign. The name of the game has changed immensely in recent years and what used to work to increase rankings quickly can now have a negative effect on your website and traffic.

High Quality Backlinks

We've all been tempted to buy 1,000 backlinks for $25 at some point (hopefully not any time recently), but don't fall for these "cheap" link building services that can have a devastating effect on your site. This is one area of SEO that shouldn't be taken lightly after the numerous Google algorithm updates in 2013 that were released to fight web spam. Search engines are constantly becoming smarter and smarter and now look for a "natural" link profile consisting of your domain name, company name, "click here", "read more", variations of keyword link text, and other generic terms in addition to your exact match keywords.

The truth of the matter is that getting high quality backlinks takes time, effort, and patience. Cheap links can only mean automated software, over-optimization of link text, link farms, blog networks, poorly spun articles, low quality directories or social bookmarking sites, and other black-hat SEO techniques that will get you into trouble during the next algorithm refresh or update. If you decide to go this route, you will eventually find yourself needing a recovery service which can be costly and take quite some time to complete depending on the severity of the situation and how many poor quality links you've accumulated.

Do it right the first time; purchase high quality backlinks that provide a natural link profile and ensure long-lasting search engine rankings and continued success.
